Fried Egg Tortilla

Fry an egg, melt the cheese, and warm your tortilla all in the same pan. Add your favorite toppings-like salsa, jalapeño pepper slices, cilantro, and green onions-and enjoy a great tasting breakfast, done in a flash. Of course, you can repeat, as many times as you like if you have company.
Prep Time: 5 mins
Cook Time: 4 mins
Total Time: 9 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons butter
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 teaspoon taco seasoning, or to taste
  • 1 1/2 ounces shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
  • 1 (6 to 7-inch) flour tortilla

Steps

  1. In a 10-inch nonstick skillet, melt butter over medium heat, and swirl the pan to coat the bottom evenly.
  2. Carefully break the egg into the buttered skillet and cook until the egg white is opaque and the egg is firm enough to turn, about 1 minute.
  3. Turn the egg over, being careful not to break the yolk; sprinkle with taco seasoning and about half of the cheese.
  4. Gently place the tortilla over the egg and cheese, and cook about 1 minute. Using a silicone egg turner, flip the egg and tortilla together.
  5. Sprinkle the remaining cheese on top of the egg, and continue cooking until the egg is done to your liking and the tortilla is warm and toasty, about 2 minutes.
  6. Slide the tortilla and egg onto a serving plate and serve warm.
